    ABOUT US

    Digitalization and online learning in the education system of our country is significantly increasing. That is why we came up with this project ‘Students Portal’ to support students in school. Having this platform in a digital system, web-based application is the ideal way to implement a student platform in a school setting.

    This project's overarching objective is to support the teaching-learning process in a classroom setting by giving the school community a collaborative and engaging platform. Our platform aims to reduce the gap between students, teachers and parents in order to improve overall efficiency of the educational system as a whole. It provides students with an easy way to access educational resources, makes teachers accessible at all times and from any location, communicates with parents quickly about their children's progress, and, most importantly, keeps the majority of the data on a reliable digital platform, eliminating time-consuming paperwork. on the completion of this project we believe this platform is capable of accelerating productivity of teachers and students
